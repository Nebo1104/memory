Transition: Stage 1 → Stage 2
REFLECTING THE SILENCE
WHY IT HAPPENED: The conversation with Grandfather led into a wall of repeating phrases, fear, and alcohol. This is exactly how the Soviet terror functioned on a family level. It forced the generation of eyewitnesses to completely erase their memory and replace it with a single, safe, short myth to survive and protect their children. Asking them for details is futile—their silence was a shield.
WHAT AWAITS YOU NEXT: To find the fragments of the shattered truth, you must leave the kitchen and enter the state archives. In the next stage, you will get access to the actual criminal investigation file from July 1941. You will have to read between the lines of the NKVD interrogation protocols, search warrants, and formal charges to see the real tragedy behind the official bureaucratic ink.
