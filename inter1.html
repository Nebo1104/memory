THE SILENCE AS A SHIELD: Grandfather’s repetitive phrases, fear, and alcohol showcase how Soviet terror functioned at the family level. Witnesses erased their own memories. They replaced them with single, safe myths to protect their children. Asking them for details is futile—their silence was a survival strategy.
UNCOVERING THE ARCHIVES: To find the shattered truth, you must enter the state archives. In 2016, I first visited the SBU (Security Service of Ukraine) reading room in person. By 2025, due to the full-scale Russian-Ukrainian war, the physical reading room was closed for safety. I requested a page-by-page digital copy of the 1941 NKVD criminal case, which I then fully digitized and processed using AI tools. You are about to step into these authentic digitized interrogation protocols, search warrants, and formal charges.
