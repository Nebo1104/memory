A Moment of Reflection
EMOTIONAL PAUSE: Interrogation protocols and family separation can be deeply emotional to witness. If you feel overwhelmed, please take a short break, stretch, or drink some water. Your well-being comes first.
THE REALITY OF THE THAW (1958): Next, you will write a rehabilitation petition as Mykola. It is crucial to look past the historical myth. Khrushchev’s Thaw was not an act of genuine reconciliation or justice. It was a calculated political tool designed to reduce severe social tension and placate the West.

Mykola does not write this letter out of a pure quest for abstract justice. Because of his father's political conviction under Art. 54-10, Mykola is heavily restricted in his civil rights. The sky is legally closed to him. He writes this petition out of survival—as the only desperate way to bypass the Soviet ban and enter a higher education institute to become a pilot.
